Color gradients and CANDELS galaxies


I use spatial-resolved deep photometry as obtained by the CANDELS survey to dissect the colors of z~1 star-forming galaxies. Quite different from their local buddies, high redshift galaxies are known to be considerably obscured by dust. As a result, before knowing well about where stars are born inside these galaxies, which is among the key topics of galaxy formation scenario, dust extinction effect must me approriately evaluated (the so-called age-dust degeneracy).
